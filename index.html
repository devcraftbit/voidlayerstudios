System Inquiries (FAQ)

Common questions regarding our titles, engine, and hardware compatibility.

Which VR headsets do your games support?
Our titles are designed for maximum compatibility across major platforms, including high-end PC VR setups and standalone mobile headsets. We utilize the MST Framework to ensure seamless performance regardless of the hardware you choose.
Do I need a large physical space to play AR titles?
Not necessarily. While a larger space offers a broader canvas for augmented elements, our adaptive spatial mapping dynamically scales the virtual environment to fit comfortably within your available room dimensions.
Is the MST Framework Builder available to the public?
Currently, the MST Framework is a proprietary tool maintained exclusively for in-house development at VoidLayer Studios. However, we are exploring options to license specialized modules to select partners in the future.
How can I participate in beta testing?
We announce all closed and open beta phases through our official news channels. Follow our updates and sign up via the forms provided during announcement periods to secure your slot.
