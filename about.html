Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms do you develop for?
We focus on top-tier VR hardware and advanced AR platforms. Our games are optimized for seamless performance across modern standalone headsets and high-end PC VR setups.
How does the MST Constructor improve the games?
The MST Constructor is our internal framework that vastly accelerates the building of complex, physics-driven environments. It allows our artists to iterate faster and maintain incredibly high visual fidelity without compromising frame rates.
Are your titles focused on multiplayer or single-player?
We craft both deeply narrative-driven single-player experiences and dynamic, social multiplayer arenas. The approach depends heavily on the specific project and the universe we are building.
